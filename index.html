About NutriScan

Smart, AI-assisted food label insights powered by open data.

NutriScan lets you quickly understand what is inside packaged foods by combining barcode scanning, the Open Food Facts database, and a lightweight AI-inspired scoring layer that uses their product data.

When you scan a product, NutriScan pulls the full ingredient list and related nutrition fields (such as NOVA processing group, nutrition grade, and ingredient-level flags like vegan or palm-oil content). A health score from 0–100 is then estimated using only this structured data, and each ingredient is color-coded to help you reason about the overall profile.

This prototype runs fully in your browser, saving recent scans locally and keeping the design intentionally simple so it works well inside coding tools like Cursor.
